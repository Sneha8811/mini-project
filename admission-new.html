Admission in Hospital or Care centers


Details


User prone to covid-19 can easily admit themselves in any hospital near their region.This website helps user to find many hospitals and care centers near their region with bed availabilty count. Either a hospital or a care center can be choosen by an user.User with high severity will be able to choose a hospital and an user with low severity will be able to choose a care center.There are numerous Hospitals and Care center list here.


Procedure to book an admission


  • Register in this website
  • Login into your account using your credentials
  • Go to Admission page
  • Fill out Admission form
  • You will be directed to list of hospital or care center page according to your severity that you have provided in the admission form
  • Select your desired hospital or care center
  • Complete your payment and the admission is booked
