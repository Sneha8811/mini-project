Executive Assistant/Bookkeeper


Details


Established small family office seeking detail oriented individual for diverse position as an Executive Assistant for partners, bookkeeping and investment tracking. Computer proficiency in MS Word, Excel, Outlook and the Internet is critical for this position. Experience in Quickbooks and knowledge of basic accounting concepts required. Superior grammar and strong written and oral communication skills necessary. Must have excellent organizational skills, a proactive work ethic and sharp attention to detail. Excellent problem solving skills and keen judgement and the ability to coordinate multiple tasks and deadlines in a fast paced environment. This position assures the successful family office since 1989 operates efficiently and effectively with the highest level of confidentiality and customer service.

  • Extremely tech savvy

  • Working knowledge of office equipment, printers and fax machines
